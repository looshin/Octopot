About Octopot

Octopotâ€™s overall mission is to provide everyone with equal access to clean hygiene. We are a non-profit organization that works with partners to distribute resources to the Seattle community and to abolish the causes of public health problems. Such projects can include products such as those under the pink tax(feminine hygiene products), working with companies to fund places for disadvantaged communities to use bathrooms, and educating the population. Our volunteer section works on finding ways to increase the access of these products for marginalized communities.

We will be present at Seattle local events where the population can stop by and interact with our organization. At these events, we will be creating fundraisers, educating, and asking for possible donations. If you are lucky you might even get some Octopot merch by stopping by one of our booths. All proceeds will go to help provide resources of hygiene for disadvantaged communities.

Some of the projects we might be introducing soon are pop-up thrift shops where we take resued clothes and repurpose them for people who might benefit from them. Some of the clothes might go towards benefiting communities directly, and other clothes might be sold in our shops. We will be asking for volunteers for the pop-up thrift stores, and the proceeds will go towards financing other projects. We are also working on connecting with portable toilet companies to create more public bathrooms in areas where finding public bathrooms are a challenge. Finally, we plan to make care packages centered on hygiene(toilet paper, menstruation, soap, etc.) that we will distribute to different organizations around the Seattle area.

Octopot is always up to suggestions to become more environmentally and societally friendly. Please message us if you have any ideas, and we will be glad to look over it. We will update our page soon with big updates such as volunteer work, and how are new projects are going.
