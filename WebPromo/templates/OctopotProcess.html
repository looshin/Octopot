Carbon to Fuel

In Geological Seconds...

We use a process known as Hydrothermal carbonization for our waste management services. This process works on many carbon-based substances and can reduce their harmful longevity into safe materials. The process requires reactors that will convert a solution of sewage into safer environmental products like biofuels, and hydrochar.

Using a hydrothermal(HTC) reactor, a solution of sewage can be heated at a specific pressure for an allotted amount of time to form HTC slurry which can later have its solids and liquids separated from each other. Leftover liquid solutions can be converted to biofuels, while the solid hydrochar from the solution can be dried, and used to help sustain our planet in multiple ways.

HTC is a remarkable product that works with numerous amounts of products. The possibilities are seemingly endless, and it doe snot just stop at sewage. Unfortunately establishing a large presence throughout governments will take a lot longer, so as a temporary solution, Octopot is attempting to offer a privatized and temporary solution for this waste removal method.

Biofuels can be used as an alternative to carbon emissions from gasoline fuel. Biofuels additionally being widely accessible will be a cheaper alternative. Furthermore, it will be ethically sourced unlike many gasoline products in the modern world.
