If you are a an organization wanting to connect with us, and want to know more about our projects please contact us at octopotofficial@gmail.com. Our current projects right now include working with organizations to provide portable toilets in areas without accessible public bathroom access. Additionally we work to repurpose clothes so we can finance the connections that might be required from our projects. Finally for organizations wanting our hygiene care packages(food banks, grocery stores, businesses, ect.), please let us know.

GOctopot will be willing to communicate with many organizations, but the most important resources it needs from different connected organizations include those that sell hygiene supplies(toilet paper, menstrual supplies, soap, ect.), portable toilet companies, and clothing entities. Octopot will be willing to accept leftover supplies as donations. Thank you for considering Octopot!
